    Grid properties
  • justify-content: align contents on main axis
  • align-content: align contents on cross axis
  • justify-items:
  • align-items: align contents on cross axis
  • justify-self: align block on main axis(on grid items)
  • align-self: align block on cross axis(on grid items)
  • place-content: justify-content,align-content shorthand
  • plane-items: justify-items,align-items shorthand
